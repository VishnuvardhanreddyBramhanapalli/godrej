About

Godrej Plots Doddaballapur is a premium plotted development in North Bangalore, offering over 500 plots on nearly 50 acres with plot sizes between 1200 and 4000 sq.ft. The community features wide roads, landscaped gardens, and 30+ lifestyle amenities, including clubhouses, sports facilities, and eco-friendly systems. Residents benefit from 24×7 security and excellent connectivity to the airport, major highways, IT parks, schools, malls, and hospitals. Backed by Godrej Properties, these plots offer both luxurious living and high investment potential in a fast-growing area.
